July 30, 2025 (10:00)

Last large update: July 29, 2025 (20:30)

This update:

Balance Changes

Unlocks and Shops

  • Coin Shop now unlocks at evolution 4 - 3 for "clicker" speciality, Rankshop at Evolution 8
  • Evolution 4 reward moved to evolution 11

Currency and Multipliers

  • Coin range (1-500 > 1-400)
  • New Multiplier: Coin Multiplier
  • Clicks multiplier and Auto multiplier are now separated
  • Less offline multiplier as rank rewards, more as speciality rewards

Offline & Autoclicker

  • Offliner: now gets offline autoclicker for free at evolution level 3 - which can normally only be bought after evo 8 (instead of 2.5% offline multiplier)
  • Offline autoclicker cost (250 RankPoints > 750 RankPoints)

Mystery Chances

  • Mystery chances changed: *2 (40% > 30%), 5* (30% > 10%), No reward (20% > 50%), Free evo (10% > 10%) - 50/50 if it is good or bad
  • You can now have multiple free evolutions at the same time

Points Exchange

  • Points exchange: Price changed (50RP > 20RP), points gained (1% > 3%), max points gain added (2% of evolution cost)

Power Prices

  • Power price: 15N+1.2^N > 15N+1.15^N (price grows slower)

Bug fixes

  • Fixed some evolution texts
  • Mystery rewards should now add to all-time points
  • Mysteryprice should now never be higher then your ammount of points (except for under 1000)
